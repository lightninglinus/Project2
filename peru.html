PERU

Welcome to the Lost World

Peru is as complex as its most intricate and exquisite weavings. Festivals mark ancient rites, the urban vanguard beams innovation and nature bestows splendid diversity.

Roam deep into the heartland of the Incas in our Peru cultural holidays travel guide, which explores the unexplained origins of Cuzco, the magnificence of Machu Picchu and the serenity of Lake Titicaca. Our interactive map helps you plan your trip through these ancient lands – with plenty of opportunities to meet the modern day inhabitants, with markets, homestays, weavig workshops and village tours.

“Peru, the richest country in the world,” invites us to discover that being rich isn’t about having the most; it’s about experiencing the most unforgettable moments. And these experiences begin on a trip to Peru, a place where the diversity and authenticity of the culture, nature and food hold the essence of life itself.
